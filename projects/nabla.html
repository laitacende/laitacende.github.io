Physics Student Society Nabla website

Physics Student Society Nabla is a Polish association at Wrocław University of Science and Technology. Their website was outdated and not appealing for a modern user.
As a member of a team, my role was more of a developer, I designed a database with a library Sleek DB. The website is written in HTML5, CSS3 with Sass, JavaScript and jQuery, and PHP. I was a part of design process as well.
